How it works

  • You describe
  • →
  • AI drafts
  • →
  • Humans review
  • →
  • Published

You never publish directly — and neither does the AI. Your report becomes a draft template, automated checks validate its structure, a human reviewer works through a quality checklist with you, and only then does the simulation join the library. That review loop is what keeps every published simulation safe, original, and worth running.

Why create one?

  • Credit. Every template carries its creator's profile and version history.
  • Reach. Published templates can be run by classrooms and teams anywhere.
  • Royalties. Verified templates are eligible for revenue share when they're run in paid settings.
